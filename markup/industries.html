Industries

Aztec Bolting has utilized more than 114 combined years of experience to provide the finest in hydraulic bolting products and services. Since 1987, we have offered the latest and greatest in bolting technology by such esteemed manufacturers as Enerpac, Norbar, Skidmore-Wilhelm, RAD, and Aztec Bolting.

We are a true pioneer in industrial bolting applications. Aztec Bolting has long served established industries such as:

  • Petrochemical and Refining
  • Oil and Gas
  • Wind Generation – Construction and Maintenance
  • Power Generation
  • Pipeline Construction and Maintenance
  • Custom Fabrication
  • Specialty Construction
Contact Aztec Bolting Today

In addition to the products available from Aztec Bolting, we also offer and request our On Site Bolting Services, CBT (Controlled Bolt Training), Torque Tool Repair, and Calibration Services. We are ISO 17025 Accredited and our calibration services meet all NIST standards.
